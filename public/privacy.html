Privacy Policy — A Dab of This, A Dab of That

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how "A Dab of This, A Dab of That" (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information. If you create an account, we collect your email address and a username you choose. This is handled through Supabase, our backend authentication and database provider. User Content. If you rate drinks, save favorites, submit community drink recipes, or write Tasting Journal entries, we store that content associated with your account. AI Bartender Chat. If you use the AI Bartender chat feature, your messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. We do not send your email address, account password, or any other personal identifying information to Anthropic as part of this feature — only the text of your chat messages and general context needed to answer your question (such as your saved drinks or ratings, if you are signed in). Age Verification. The App requires users to confirm they are 21 years of age or older before granting access, due to alcohol-related content. We do not store the specific birthdate entered; we only store a local confirmation that age verification was completed.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide and maintain your account
  • Save and sync your favorites, ratings, and journal entries across sessions
  • Allow community rating and drink submission features to function
  • Power the AI Bartender chat feature
  • Process your one-time Premium purchase, if applicable

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting
  • Anthropic — AI chat functionality (Claude API)
  • Vercel — web application hosting
  • Google Play Billing — processing in-app purchases (Android)

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data on their platforms.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Your account information and user content are retained as long as your account remains active. You may request account deletion by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

This App is intended for users 21 years of age or older due to its alcohol-related content. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Update or delete your account information by contacting us
  • Stop using the AI Bartender chat feature at any time without affecting other app functionality
  • Use the App without creating an account, with limited features (browsing recipes, viewing the Spirit Guide, and using the Bar Card)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
